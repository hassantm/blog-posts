---
title: "Why I'm writing this"
description: "A brief explanation of what this is and what it isn't."
pubDate: 2026-03-29
---

I've been meaning to write for a while. Not in an "I should really exercise more" kind of way — I genuinely have things I want to think through in public. Writing helps me figure out what I actually think.

Most of what I want to write about is being a generalist. I've had a career that zigzags: drug discovery, IT implementation, operations. No single deep speciality. For a long time I read that as a weakness. I'm less sure now, and I want to work out why.

This blog is the working out. No editorial calendar, no "content strategy." Just writing when I have something worth saying.

We'll see how that goes.
